<p><ul><li>Single Point Rod Extensometer</li><li>Multipoint Rod Extensometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rod extensometers are used for measuring linear displacement in various applications. Single point rod extensometers have one measuring point and are suitable for simple, straight-forward measurements. On the other hand, multipoint rod extensometers have multiple measuring points, allowing for more complex and detailed measurements over a larger area. Both types of extensometers are commonly used in geotechnical and structural monitoring to measure small deformations in the ground, rock, or structures.</p></p>
