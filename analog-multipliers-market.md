<p><p>Analog multipliers are classified into three market types based on the number of quadrants they operate in. Single quadrant multipliers only operate in the positive or negative range, while two quadrant multipliers operate in both positive and negative ranges but cannot output negative values. On the other hand, four quadrant multipliers can operate in all four quadrants and output both positive and negative values. Each market type caters to different applications and requirements within the analog multiplier industry.</p></p>
