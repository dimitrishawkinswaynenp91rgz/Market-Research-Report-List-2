<p><p>Platform elevators are a type of elevator that is used for transporting goods and people between different levels of a building. There are three main types of platform elevators: manual, electric, and hydraulic. Manual platform elevators are operated by hand and are typically used in smaller buildings. Electric platform elevators are powered by electricity and are commonly found in medium-sized buildings. Hydraulic platform elevators use hydraulic power to operate and are often used in larger, more complex buildings. Each type of platform elevator has its own advantages and disadvantages depending on the specific needs of the building.</p></p>
