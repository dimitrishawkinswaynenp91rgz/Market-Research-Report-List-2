<p><p>Manual pull stations are devices used to manually activate a fire alarm system. In the single action market, pulling a handle or pushing a button is all that is required to activate the alarm. In the dual action market, two separate actions are needed to activate the alarm, typically pushing a cover to access the activation mechanism and then pulling a handle or pushing a button. Single action pull stations are simpler and quicker to activate, while dual action pull stations can help prevent false alarms.</p></p>
