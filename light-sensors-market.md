<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Light Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Light Sensor</li><li>Non-Contact Light Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Contact light sensors are devices that rely on physical contact with the surface or object being measured to detect changes in light intensity. These sensors are commonly used in applications where direct contact with the measurement target is possible, such as in industrial automation or robotics.</p><p>Non-contact light sensors, on the other hand, use remote sensing technology to detect changes in light levels without the need for physical contact with the target. These sensors are often used in applications where contact is not feasible, such as in medical devices or security systems. Both types of light sensors play a crucial role in various industries and applications.</p></p>
