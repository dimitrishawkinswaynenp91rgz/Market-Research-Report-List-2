<p><p>On-premise ERP systems are deployed directly on a company's hardware and servers, allowing for more control and customization but requiring higher maintenance costs. Cloud-based ERP systems are hosted by a third-party provider and accessed through the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and lower upfront costs. On-premise ERP systems are preferred by large enterprises with complex systems, while cloud-based ERP systems are becoming more popular among small and medium-sized businesses looking for cost-effective solutions with easy access to real-time data and updates.</p></p>
