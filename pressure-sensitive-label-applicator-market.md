<p><p>Pressure sensitive label applicators are available in two main market types: automatic and semi-automatic. Automatic applicators are designed for high-volume production environments where efficiency and speed are key. These machines can apply labels onto products rapidly and continuously without the need for manual intervention. On the other hand, semi-automatic applicators are better suited for smaller scale operations that require more flexibility and control. Operators must manually feed products into the machine for labeling. Both types of applicators help businesses improve labeling accuracy and consistency.</p></p>
