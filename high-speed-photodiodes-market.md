<p><ul><li>Silicon Photodiodes</li><li>InGaAs Photodiodes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High speed photodiodes are photoelectric sensors that convert light signals into electrical currents at a fast rate. Silicon photodiodes utilize silicon as the semiconductor material and are commonly used in visible light applications. InGaAs photodiodes, on the other hand, use Indium Gallium Arsenide as the semiconductor material and are preferred for near-infrared light detection. Both types of photodiodes are essential in various industries such as telecommunications, automotive, and healthcare for their high speed and accuracy in detecting light signals.</p></p>
