<p><p>Agriculture pumps market includes various types such as centrifugal pumps, mixed-flow pumps, and axial-flow pumps. Centrifugal pumps are commonly used for irrigation and water supply due to their high flow rates. Mixed-flow pumps are suitable for applications requiring higher pressure and flow rates, such as flood irrigation. Axial-flow pumps are ideal for transferring water over long distances with minimal head loss. Other types of pumps in the agriculture market include diaphragm pumps and submersible pumps, each tailored to specific agricultural needs.</p></p>
