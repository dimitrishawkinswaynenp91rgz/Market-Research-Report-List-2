<p><strong>The LCD Brightness Enhancement Films (BEF) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Normal Prism Sheet</li><li>Multi-Functional Prism Sheet</li><li>Micro-Lens Film</li><li>Reflective Polarizer (DBEF)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LCD Brightness Enhancement Films (BEF) market includes four main types: Normal Prism Sheet, Multi-Functional Prism Sheet, Micro-Lens Film, and Reflective Polarizer (DBEF). Normal Prism Sheet is a basic type that enhances brightness by bending and directing light. Multi-Functional Prism Sheet offers additional features like glare reduction and privacy protection. Micro-Lens Film consists of tiny lenses that focus light for improved brightness. Reflective Polarizer (DBEF) reflects and recycles polarized light, increasing overall brightness and reducing power consumption in LCD displays.</p></p>
