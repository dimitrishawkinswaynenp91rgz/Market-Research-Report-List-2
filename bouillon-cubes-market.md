<p><p>Bouillon cubes are popular soup and broth flavoring agents available in various forms such as cubes, granules, powder, and paste. Cubes are solid blocks of concentrated broth that dissolve in hot water, while granules are smaller particles that are quickly dissolved. Powdered bouillon is a fine, dry form that can be easily sprinkled or mixed into dishes. Bouillon paste is a concentrated form that offers a more intense flavor and is often used in professional kitchens. Each form caters to different preferences and cooking needs.</p></p>
