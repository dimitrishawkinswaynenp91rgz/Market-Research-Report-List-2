<p><strong>The Waveguide Type Optical Display Element Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Holographic Waveguides</li><li>Reflective Waveguide</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Waveguide Type Optical Display Element Market comprises two main types: Holographic Waveguides and Reflective Waveguides. Holographic Waveguides use diffraction to project images onto the viewer's eye, creating high-quality, immersive visuals. Reflective Waveguides, on the other hand, use mirrors to reflect images into the viewer's field of vision, offering a bright and clear display. Both types of waveguides are popular in augmented reality and mixed reality devices due to their ability to deliver vibrant and lifelike visual experiences.</p></p>
