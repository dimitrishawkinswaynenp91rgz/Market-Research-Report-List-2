<p><p>CPU card chips are commonly found in two types in the market: contact and contactless. Contact CPU cards require physical contact with a card reader for data transfer, while contactless cards use radio-frequency identification technology for wireless communication between the card and reader. Contact cards are commonly used in credit and debit cards, while contactless cards are popular in smart payment systems and access control applications. Both types offer secure and efficient solutions for various industries.</p></p>
