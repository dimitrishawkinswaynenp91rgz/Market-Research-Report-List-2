<p><p>Endo bags come in two main market types: detachable and non-detachable. Detachable endo bags allow for easy removal of the specimen after a procedure, providing convenience and efficiency for healthcare professionals. On the other hand, non-detachable endo bags are securely sealed and do not have the option for removal, ensuring containment of the specimen within the bag. Each type has its own advantages and is chosen based on the specific needs of the healthcare setting and procedure requirements.</p></p>
