<p><p>Silicon carbide varistors come in two main types: tube varistors and disc varistors. Tube varistors are cylindrical in shape and are often used in high voltage applications due to their ability to handle high levels of energy. Disc varistors, on the other hand, are flat disc-shaped components that are commonly used in low to medium voltage circuits. Both types of varistors provide effective protection against voltage surges and are widely used in various industries for ensuring the safety and proper functioning of electronic devices.</p></p>
