<p><p>Analog pressure gauges can be categorized into two main types: Precision pressure gauges and general pressure gauges. Precision pressure gauges are designed to provide high accuracy and reliability in measuring pressure in precise applications such as laboratory settings or industrial processes where a high level of precision is required.
