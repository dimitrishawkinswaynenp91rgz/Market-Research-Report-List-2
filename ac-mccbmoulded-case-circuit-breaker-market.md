<p><p>AC MCCB (Moulded Case Circuit Breaker) market types include Thermal Magnetic MCCB and Electronic Trip MCCB. Thermal Magnetic MCCBs use a bimetallic strip to provide thermal protection and a magnetic trip mechanism for short circuit protection. Electronic Trip MCCBs use electronic sensors to trip the circuit breaker in case of overload or short circuit. Both types provide reliable protection against electrical faults and are commonly used in industrial and commercial applications for efficient and safe distribution of electrical power.</p></p>
