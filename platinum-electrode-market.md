<p><ul><li>Counter Electrode</li><li>Single Electrode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Platinum electrode market is divided into two main types based on their function: counter electrode and single electrode. Counter electrodes are used in conjunction with a reference electrode to complete an electrical circuit in electrochemical measurements. Single electrodes, on the other hand, can stand alone and are commonly used for various applications such as electroplating, fuel cells, and sensors. Both types of platinum electrodes play crucial roles in conducting accurate and reliable electrochemical experiments in various industries.</p></p>
