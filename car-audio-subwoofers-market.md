<p><p>Powered subwoofers are self-amplified and do not require an external amplifier. They are compact and easier to install, making them a popular choice for smaller cars. On the other hand, passive subwoofers require an external amplifier to power them. They are larger in size and provide more power and better sound quality. Passive subwoofers are preferred for larger vehicles or for those seeking a more customized audio setup. Both types offer options to enhance the bass in car audio systems.</p></p>
