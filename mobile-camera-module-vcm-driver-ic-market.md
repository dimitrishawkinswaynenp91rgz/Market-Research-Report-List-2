<p><strong>The Mobile Camera Module VCM Driver IC Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open-Loop VCM Driver IC</li><li>Closed-Loop VCM Driver IC</li><li>Optical Anti-Shake (OIS) VCM Driver IC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mobile Camera Module VCM Driver IC market consists of three main types: Open-Loop, Closed-Loop, and Optical Anti-Shake (OIS) VCM Driver ICs. Open-Loop VCM Driver ICs provide basic control of the lens movement, while Closed-Loop VCM Driver ICs offer more precise control by using feedback to adjust the lens position. Optical Anti-Shake (OIS) VCM Driver ICs reduce image blur by stabilizing the lens to compensate for handheld motion. Each type caters to different needs in the mobile camera module market, offering varying levels of functionality and performance.</p></p>
