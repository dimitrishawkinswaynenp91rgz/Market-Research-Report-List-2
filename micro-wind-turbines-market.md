<p><p>Micro wind turbines are small-scale wind turbines designed for residential, commercial, or industrial use. There are two main types of micro wind turbines in the market: Horizontal Axis Wind Turbine (HAWT) and Vertical Axis Wind Turbine (VAWT). HAWT blades rotate around a horizontal axis, while VAWT blades rotate around a vertical axis. HAWTs are more common and efficient in higher wind speeds, while VAWTs are more suitable for urban areas with inconsistent wind direction. Both types offer renewable energy solutions for off-grid locations or to supplement traditional power sources.</p></p>
