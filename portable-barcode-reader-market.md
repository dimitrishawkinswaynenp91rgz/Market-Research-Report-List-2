<p><strong>The Portable Barcode Reader Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laser Barcode Reader</li><li>Linear Imager Barcode Reader</li><li>2D Imager Barcode Reader</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Portable Barcode Reader Market offers three main types of barcode readers: Laser Barcode Reader, Linear Imager Barcode Reader, and 2D Imager Barcode Reader. Laser Barcode Readers use a red diode laser to read barcodes, making them ideal for long-range scanning. Linear Imager Barcode Readers use a CCD sensor to capture barcode data line by line, making them great for damaged or poorly printed barcodes. 2D Imager Barcode Readers use a camera to capture both 1D and 2D barcodes, offering versatility and the ability to read barcodes from mobile devices.</p></p>
