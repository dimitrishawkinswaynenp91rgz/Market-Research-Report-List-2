<p><ul><li>Digital</li><li>Analog</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Theatre consoles can be categorized into digital and analog markets based on the technology they use. Digital theatre consoles utilize digital signal processing to control various lighting, sound, and visual effects elements of a performance. They offer more flexibility and advanced features compared to analog consoles. Analog theatre consoles, on the other hand, operate using traditional electrical signals and controls. While they may be simpler and more straightforward to use, they lack the advanced capabilities and customization options that digital consoles provide.</p></p>
