<p><p>Evaporator and condenser coils are essential components in air conditioning and refrigeration systems. The market for these coils can be categorized into three main types. Tubed coils consist of a series of tubes through which the refrigerant flows, providing effective heat transfer. Finned or plated coils feature additional fins or plates attached to the tubes, increasing the surface area for improved heat exchange. Combined coils combine elements of both tubed and finned designs, offering a balance of efficiency and cost-effectiveness in various cooling applications.</p></p>
