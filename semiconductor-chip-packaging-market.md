<p><strong>The Semiconductor Chip Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fan-Out Wafer-Level Packaging(FO WLP)</li><li>Fan-In Wafer-Level Packaging(FI WLP)</li><li>Flip Chip(FC)</li><li>2.5D/3D</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor chip packaging includes various types such as Fan-Out Wafer-Level Packaging (FO WLP), Fan-In Wafer-Level Packaging (FI WLP), Flip Chip (FC), and 2.5D/3D packaging. FO WLP involves expanding the chip size beyond the wafer size, while FI WLP involves shrinking the chip size. Flip Chip technology directly connects the chip to the substrate, allowing for higher performance. 2.5D/3D packaging involves stacking multiple chips together to increase functionality and performance in a smaller footprint. Each packaging type offers unique advantages for different applications in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
