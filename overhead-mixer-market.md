<p><p>Constant Rate market for overhead mixers refers to a market where the mixer operates at a set speed continuously without any adjustments. On the other hand, Controllable Rate market refers to a market where the mixer's speed can be adjusted as needed to meet specific requirements. Constant Rate mixers are ideal for simple mixing tasks where a consistent speed is sufficient, while Controllable Rate mixers are more versatile and can be tailored to different mixing needs.</p></p>
