<p><strong>The Audio Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Analog Output</li><li>Digital Output</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Audio sensors can be divided into two main types based on their output signal: analog output and digital output. </p><p>Analog output audio sensors produce continuous voltage or current signals that vary in response to changes in sound levels. They are commonly used in applications where precise audio measurements are required.</p><p>On the other hand, digital output audio sensors convert sound signals into digital data, typically in the form of binary code. This type of sensor is easier to interface with digital systems and offers greater accuracy and reliability in capturing audio data.</p></p>
