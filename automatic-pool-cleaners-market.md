<p><ul><li>Negative Pressure Type</li><li>Positive Pressure Type</li><li>Robotic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic pool cleaners come in three main types: Negative Pressure Type, Positive Pressure Type, and Robotic Type. Negative Pressure Type cleaners work by attaching to the pool's filtration system, using the suction power to clean the pool. Positive Pressure Type cleaners use the pool's water pressure to move around and clean the pool. Robotic Type cleaners are self-contained units that operate independently from the pool's filtration system, using their own power source to clean the pool efficiently. Each type offers different benefits and options for pool owners to choose from.</p></p>
