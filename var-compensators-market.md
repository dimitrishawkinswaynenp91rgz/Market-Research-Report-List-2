<p><ul><li>TCR-typed SVC</li><li>MCR- typed SVC</li><li>TSC- typed SVC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Var Compensators are devices used in power systems to regulate voltage and reactive power flow. There are three main types of Var Compensators in the market: TCR-typed SVC, MCR-typed SVC, and TSC-typed SVC. TCR-typed SVCs use thyristor-controlled reactors to regulate reactive power flow. MCR-typed SVCs use mechanically switched capacitors and reactors for the same purpose. TSC-typed SVCs utilize thyristor-switched capacitors to achieve voltage and reactive power control. Each type of Var Compensator offers unique capabilities and advantages, catering to different needs in power system applications.</p></p>
