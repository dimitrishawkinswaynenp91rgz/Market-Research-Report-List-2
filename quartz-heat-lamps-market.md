<p><p>Quartz heat lamps are categorized into three types based on the wavelength of infrared radiation they emit: short wave, medium wave, and long wave. Short wave lamps provide intense heat quickly, making them ideal for heating applications that require rapid response times. Medium wave lamps offer a balance between quick heating and energy efficiency. Long wave lamps emit gentle, consistent heat over a larger area, making them suitable for spaces where maintaining a constant temperature is important. Each type of lamp caters to specific heating needs and preferences.</p></p>
