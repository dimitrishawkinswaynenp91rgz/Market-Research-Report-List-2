<p><strong>The Inkjet Disc Printers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Ink Technology</li><li>Drop on Demand Technology</li><li>Head Design</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inkjet disc printers come in various types based on technology used for printing discs. Continuous Ink Technology involves a continuous flow of ink to the print head, providing efficient and consistent printing. Drop on Demand Technology only releases ink droplets as needed, reducing waste and ensuring precise printing. The Head Design market focuses on the design and functionality of the print head, determining the quality and speed of printing on discs. These factors play a significant role in the performance of inkjet disc printers.</p></p>
