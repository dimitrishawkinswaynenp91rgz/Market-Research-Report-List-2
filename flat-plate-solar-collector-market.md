<p><p>Flat plate solar collector is a type of solar thermal collector that absorbs sunlight and converts it into thermal energy for heating water or air. It consists of a flat absorber plate made of a dark material, a transparent cover, and an insulated backing. The absorber plate absorbs sunlight and heats up, transferring the heat to a fluid that circulates through the collector and is then used for heating purposes.</p><p>The flat plate solar collector market is experiencing steady growth, driven by increasing awareness about the benefits of renewable energy sources and the rising demand for sustainable heating solutions.
