<p><ul><li>Remote Eye Tracking</li><li>Mobile Eye Tracking</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Eye tracking systems can be categorized into two main types: remote eye tracking and mobile eye tracking. Remote eye tracking systems use stationary cameras to track and record a person's eye movements and gaze patterns, typically in a controlled laboratory setting. On the other hand, mobile eye tracking systems utilize wearable devices or glasses equipped with small cameras to capture eye movements in real-world environments. Both types of eye tracking systems are used for various applications such as market research, usability testing, and human-computer interaction studies.</p></p>
