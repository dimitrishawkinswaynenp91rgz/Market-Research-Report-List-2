<p><p>Wireless garage door openers are available in two main types: Wi-Fi based and Bluetooth based. Wi-Fi based openers connect to the internet and can be controlled remotely through a smartphone app from anywhere with an internet connection. On the other hand, Bluetooth based openers connect directly to a smartphone or other device through a short-range wireless connection. Both types offer convenience and ease of use, but Wi-Fi based openers provide more flexibility in terms of remote access and control.</p></p>
