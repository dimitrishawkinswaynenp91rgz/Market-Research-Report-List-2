<p><p>Flow regulators are devices used to control the flow of liquids or gases within a system. Volumetric flow regulators measure the volume of fluid passing through the system over a set period of time, ensuring a consistent flow rate. On the other hand, Mass flow regulators measure the mass of the fluid passing through the system, providing more accurate control over the flow rate. Both types of flow regulators are essential in various industries such as healthcare, manufacturing, and chemical processing to maintain precise and efficient operations.</p></p>
