<p><p>The Inertial Navigation System market consists of various types, including Mechanical Gyro, Ring Laser Gyro, Fiber Optics Gyro, MEMS, and Others. Mechanical Gyro systems rely on spinning masses for navigation, while Ring Laser Gyro systems use laser beams to measure angular velocity. Fiber Optics Gyros utilize the interference of light beams in optical fibers for navigation. MEMS systems incorporate micro-electromechanical components for accurate navigation. Other systems in the market may include specific technologies or combinations of existing technologies for navigation purposes.</p></p>
