<p><p>External rotor motors are commonly classified by the type of current they operate on, either AC (alternating current) or DC (direct current). AC external rotor motors are typically used in applications that require constant speed and are ideal for devices like fans, pumps, and compressors. DC external rotor motors, on the other hand, are more commonly found in applications where variable speed control is required, such as in robotics, industrial machinery, and automotive systems. Both types of motors have their own unique advantages and are utilized in various industries based on their specific requirements.</p></p>
