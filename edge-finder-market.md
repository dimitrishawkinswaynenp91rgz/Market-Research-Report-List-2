<p><p>Edge Finder is a tool used in machining to locate the edges of a workpiece. There are two main types of Edge Finders in the market, Mechanical Type and Photoelectric Type. Mechanical Edge Finders use physical contact to detect edges, while Photoelectric Edge Finders use light sensors. Mechanical Edge Finders are commonly used for manual machining applications, while Photoelectric Edge Finders are preferred for automated machining processes due to their non-contact method. Both types offer precision in locating edges for accurate machining operations.</p></p>
