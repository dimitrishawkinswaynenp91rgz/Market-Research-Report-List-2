<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Preaction Sprinkler Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non Interlock</li><li>Single Interlock</li><li>Double Interlock</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Preaction sprinkler systems are designed to prevent false alarms and water damage by having a pre-action control panel that must be activated before water can flow through the system. In a non-interlock system, the control panel must be manually activated. In a single interlock system, the control panel is activated by a smoke detector or heat sensor. In a double interlock system, both the detection device and the control panel must be activated before water can flow. Each type offers varying levels of protection and customization for different applications.</p></p>
